Self-hosted by design

Deploy it in your own environment in minutes

wardn ships as a single container set you run wherever your infrastructure lives. Connect your existing tools over private networking — nothing is routed through us.

  • Full data sovereignty. Metrics, logs and secrets stay inside your perimeter — meet compliance and residency requirements out of the box.
  • Bring your own models. Point the AI assistant at your own LLM endpoint, a cloud provider, or a fully local model. No mandatory external calls.
  • Connect what you already run. Native MCP integrations for your observability, infra, ticketing and comms tools — connect once, use everywhere.
  • You own the upgrade cadence. Pin versions, run in air-gapped networks, and audit every change. No surprise SaaS updates.
